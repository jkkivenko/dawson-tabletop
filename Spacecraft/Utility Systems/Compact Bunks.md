# Description

Whether to cut costs or because the situation requires it, the Passenger Bunks aboard this Spacecraft have had their entertainment programs removed in order to fit more people in the same space.

# Effects

The Passenger Bunks' effect becomes the following:

| Power Draw | Effect |
| -----------|--------|
| 0 | The ship's Passenger Capacity is increased by 20. |
