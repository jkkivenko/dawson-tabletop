# Description

If you're travelling between star empires or interstellar unions, knowing exactly where the borders are is essential so you don't have to pay border taxes or get caught with contraband goods.

# Enrich

You rigorously study interstellar borders. Depending on the situation, you are much more Familiar with interplanetary borders than most people, to the point that you immediately know the territory of important interstellar players.
