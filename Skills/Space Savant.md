# Description

Star charts, flight paths, shipping lanes - who needs 'em!? You've got everything you need for complex interstellar flight plans right here in your brain (or another similar organ). 

# Enrich

You've seen enough star charts to build a (mostly incomplete) map of  the galaxy in your head. Depending on the situation, you are more Familiar with stars, planets, and interstellar objects than most people.
