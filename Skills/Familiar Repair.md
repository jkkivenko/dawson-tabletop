#WIP #skill 

# Description

You're starting to notice that you're basically the only thing holding this Spacecraft together, and you've gotten used to patching holes and replacing burnt-out laser coils on this junkyard that you call a Spacecraft.

# Effects

You gain the following activity:

## Activity: Familiar Repair

**Requirement: You have the required materials and tools for the job.**

You pull out your tools and get to work repairing a familiar mechanical device. In `10 minutes`, you can spend `75` credits worth of raw materials, spare parts, and mechanical components to attempt to repair a damaged device, system, or technological lifeform with which you are extremely familiar. If your familiarity with the target device is Familiar, Second Nature, or Like the Back of Your Hand, you restore `20` Integrity to the damaged device up to its maximum Integrity  and consume the materials.
