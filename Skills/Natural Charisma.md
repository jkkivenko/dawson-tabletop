# Description

You have a natural confidence that people find equal parts charming and disarming. Whenever you're in public, it seems like people want to chat with you. To be honest, it probably gets annoying, but you can use it to your advantage when the time is right.

# Enrich

You have a knack for getting to know people and exchanging favours. In fact, it seems like you always have some way to get out of a jam. Depending on the situation, you are more Familiar with strangers and acquaintances than most people.
