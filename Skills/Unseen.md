# Description

Even in the world of scanners and targeting sensors, sometimes the best way to avoid notice is just to hide. You might be a secret agent, a conniving criminal, or a stealthy assassin, or you might just recognise the utility of not being noticed. Regardless, people would applaud your hiding skills if they could find you.

# Effects

When you Hide, the Evasion bonus is increased by `10%`.
