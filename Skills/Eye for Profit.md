#WIP #skill #enrich

# Description

You might be a businessperson, merchant, or just someone who hates getting swindled. Regardless, you can tell something's value just by giving it a glance.

# Enrich

You have spent long enough doing business to know something's worth just by looking at it. Depending on the situation, you are more Familiar with the value of goods and services than most people, although a lying merchant might get an Evasion chance against your appraisal attempts.
