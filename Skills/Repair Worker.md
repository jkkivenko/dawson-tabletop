#WIP #skill 

# Description

You consider yourself handy enough to fix a plasma coupling, polaron matrix, or broken toaster. More than just a useful skill, repairing technology can be the difference between life and death when you're stranded in deep space.

# Effects

You gain the following activity:

## Activity: Repair Device

**Requirement: You have the required materials and tools for the job.**

You pull out your tools and get to work. In `30 minutes`, you can spend `100` credits worth of raw materials, spare parts, and mechanical components to attempt to repair a damaged device, system, or technological lifeform. This attempt has a `60%` Accuracy, consuming the materials regardless of whether you succeed.

On a success, you restore `10` Integrity to the damaged device up to its maximum Integrity. 

Based on your Familiarity with the device, your Accuracy may increase. Likewise, if the device is unfamiliar to you, it may gain an Evasion chance against your repairs.
