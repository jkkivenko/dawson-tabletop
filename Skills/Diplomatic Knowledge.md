#WIP #skill #enrich

# Description

Whether or not you're actually a diplomat it always pays to know what the nations and planets of the galaxy are up to - alliances, wars, current events. You just so happen to be well-versed in the goings-on of the interplanetary powers in the galaxy.

# Enrich

You keep up with the local news when it comes to past or present diplomatic conflicts and events. Depending on the situation, you are more Familiar with intergalactic conflicts and alliances than most people.
