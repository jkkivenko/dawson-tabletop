#WIP #skill 

# Description

Being a hacker yourself, you know all the tricks that hackers use to break in and cause problems. Because of that, you can intercept a hacking attempt in progress, using your own knowledge of computer systems to deny the hacker access.

# Effects

You gain the Counterhack action:

## Action: Counterhack

You stop a hack on your systems, making sure the enemy hacker doesn't gain access. Any Hack or Shutdown Code A1TF4 attempt against a computer you are adjacent to immediately fails.

If the system is not adjacent to you but you are operating a computer connected to the system's network, your Counterhack has `70%` Accuracy, only blocking the attempt on a success.
