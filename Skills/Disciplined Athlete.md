# Description

You take very good care of your body. Maybe you're a manual labourer, you need to be tough to protect yourself, you're a trained athlete, or you just like the way it feels to exercise. Everyone has their reasons, and yours allow you to perform feats of strength befitting someone of your discipline.

# Effect

You gain the Disciplined Athlete passive:

# Passive: Disciplined Athlete

You gain a `+10%` Accuracy bonus when you use Effort or Get to Work to perform a physical activity (i.e. lifting a heavy object, escaping bonds, climbing a rope, etc.).
