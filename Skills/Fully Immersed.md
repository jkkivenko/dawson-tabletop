#WIP #skill 

# Description

You know how to pass yourself off as a native in a culture you've studied. If you have used Observe Culture or you know the culture well, you and your allies can don disguises and convince natives that you are members of their society.

# Effects

You gain the Uh, We Just Moved Here activity:

## Activity: Uh, We Just Moved Here

With `30` minutes of tailoring clothes and applying makeup, you can pass off up to `5` lifeforms as members of another society. They resemble standard civilians, although you can improve their perceived wealth with additional time and resources. You cannot impersonate the looks of an existing member of the society with these disguises.
