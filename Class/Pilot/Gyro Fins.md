#WIP #pilot #spacecraft-action 

# Description

While a Tripod comes in handy on the ground, your Blast Pack comes equipped with carefully tuned gyroscopic stabilisation to increase your accuracy while in flight as well.

# Effects

You gain the following:

## Passive: Gyro Fins

**Requirement: You chose to Hover this turn.**

You gain `+10%` Accuracy and `+10%` Range for weapon attacks.

## Action: Strafing Run

**Requirement: You are operating a Helm Workstation.**

You expertly line up your Spacecraft's weapons. For the next `10s`, your Spacecraft's Weapons gain a `+10%` Accuracy bonus.
