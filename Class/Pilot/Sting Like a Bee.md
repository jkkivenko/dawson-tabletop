#WIP #pilot 

# Description

While in the air, you swoop down and unleash a devastating barrage of weapons fire on an unexpecting enemy.

# Effects

You gain the Sting Like a Bee action.

## Action: Sting Like a Bee

**Requirement: You chose to Hover this turn.**

Choose a target within `20m`. You immediately fly overtop of the target (or to an adjacent space, if the space is occupied) and make a weapon attack using each weapon you are holding. The target suffers a `-10%` Evasion chance against these attacks.
